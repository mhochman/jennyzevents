Chris + Elisa

Ceremony: St. James Cathedral

Reception: Georgetown Ballroom

Hotel: Renaissance Seattle

Make Up: Hanna Mazur

Photographer: Alexa Rae Photography

Videography: Ashley L. Productions

DJ: Injoy Entertainment

Flowers: floressence

Cake: Jacqui’s Cakes

Caricatures: Over the Line Art

Dessert: Pop-Up Bike Pops


Looking through Elisa + Chris’s photos and thinking back on their wedding day makes me smile so big. Elisa told me about a year out from the wedding that her vision for the evening was for everyone to be dancing and having a good time. And the reception was just that. Every detail was focused on fun – from the playlist powered by “dancing shoes” to the caricatures to the Pop-Up Bike Pops!
