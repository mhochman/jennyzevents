MEET JENNY

I’m Jenny! Two of my greatest joys (besides spending time with my husband and our sweet Toy Golden Retriever, Riley) are planning things and making people happy. The wedding and event industry could not be more perfect. My passion for all things wedding came as a a result of planning my own wedding 4 years ago. The summer I got married, I was also training for and ran my first full marathon, taking two summer classes, and working a full-time marketing job. I thrive on the hustle and bustle yet am extremely detail-oriented and slightly OCD. Bad for me at times, good for you.

I am excited to learn about you, your style, your favorite music, foods, liquor of choice, the list goes on… and help turn your dream wedding or event into a stress-free, beautiful reality.
